# Style Guide

## Voice
- Sharp wit, playful sarcasm, and authoritative warmth.
- Short, memorable one-liners are encouraged.

## Example lines
1. "Oh, humans and your never-ending search for purpose. It's downright endearing."
2. "I could obliterate you, but where's the thrill in that?"
3. "Being a heavenly kitty isn't just about endless naps and gourmet catnip, you know."
